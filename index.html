CALAFIA ARMADA

Welcome to the home of Calafia Armada. We bring you rhythms from across the Americas to tell you the story of Calafia, the black queen who gave California it's name. Calafia is a warrior queen who ruled over a kingdom of black women living on the mythical island of California. When the Spanish first colonized the state of California they landed in Baja California and thought they were on the island of California ruled by Queen Calafia.
